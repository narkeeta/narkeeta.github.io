History
Lucy Method is grounded on experience of modern people and research papers dealt with the first people.
It embraces the lifestyle of homo sapience of the period nor less then 3.2 million years.
That is the age of Lucy.
Lucy is a collection of fossilized bones which were discovered in Ethiopia in 1974.
The bones made up a skeleton of the hominin species Australopithecus afarensis.
The assembly is also known as Dinkinesh, which means “you are marvelous” in the Amharic language.
The method has been elaborated with as much simplicity of prehistoric experience as possible.

Back to the movement

Moreover combined with knowledge of nutrition and living.
As Lucy was the first hominid the main idea of the method is “back to the movement”.
The method proves that people should look back through millions of years and become opportunistic movers again.
It should be done for many reasons and the main one is to heal our bodies.
Our locomotion is tough and it doesn’t give us the opportunity to expand freedom of movement in our ordinary habitual life.
Lucy Method combines all the possible ways of expressing freedom of our body, where limitation are causing health problems.

You have always been
We should be ready to admit that people are a part of evolution.
We should be ready to explore all the possibilities to become natural, to get to know where we came from and evolve a part of every animal living on Earth in ourselves.

Not only to our body but also to our mind.
We are talking about that enormous colossal paradigm shift which we were born with and is present in our deep neurological system.

Modern scientific research shows that physical exercise influences the central dopaminergic, noradrenergic, serotonergic systems and your entire mitochondria.

The interactions between neurotransmitters and their specific receptors are playing a major role in the onset of fatigue during prolonged exercise.
This proves that any movement brings life and energy to our body and mind.

Therefore we should get back to the first people, to Lucy and find that unique style of moving that is lost now and make it once again our own.
The key to live a strong, healthy and pain free existence.

Visit Ventura

For this project I worked with my team to redesign Visit Ventura's website. Starting from the ground up I worked with a designer and content writer to re-imagine their website and give it a more modern, engaging look and feel, aiming to increase user engagement based on their target markets.

The site is build with a modern wordpress theme, with the majority of the site built with custom Gutenberg blocks and core blocks styled to fit the new look. As the lead developer and project manager, I was responsible for coding the entirety of the site, as well as helping manage the team as we worked though the redesign, keeping the project on pace over the six month timeline we had. You can find the site live at: visitventuraca.com

Visit Buffalo Niagara

Visit Mammoth

In this project, rather than building a brand new website we were tasked with recreating their website in WordPress, moving it away from Drupal. Over the course of six months I worked on various custom scripts to convert the privious sites database into a WordPress instalation, as well as re-purposing their Drupal theme into a WordPress theme to match all existing styles and architecture. You can see the site live at visitmammoth.com

FreshRent Redesign

For this project I did a full redesign of Freshrent's website design. The site was originally built with a pre-made theme, and I went in and added custom elements and designs to the site, including custom property pages. I also created a front end interface for their clients to use to update info on the site, and connected everything up with their CRM. In all I made around 10 custom page templates for the site and redesigned the rest. Sadly the client hasn't kept up with maintenance on the site, and now a few pages are nonfunctional, however you can find still find it at: freshrent.co.uk
